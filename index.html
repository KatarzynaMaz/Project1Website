Welcome to Kopernik Observatory. The premiere obseratory of NY.

The Kopernik Observatory & Science Center opened in 1974, built by Kopernik Society of Brooe County, to commemorate the 500th anniversary of Mikolaj Kopernik, the man know as father of modern Astronomy.Since that time, hundreds of thousands of children and adults have experience the wonders of science thought the Observatory's programs.
